Routes and Schedules

OCC Transport has various routes that serve the Binghamton University community. Below are our routes, their most popular stop and the main streets they travel on. Click on any route to view its routing, stops, and schedule!

All buses at the University Downtown Center return to the main campus.

WS

Services Floral Avenue in Johnson City, Main Street in Binghamton, and the University Downtown Center in Downtown Binghamton.

MS

Services Main Street in Johnson City & Binghamton, the Pharmacy & Nursing Schools, and the University Downtown Center in Downtown Binghamton.

DCL

Services Bunn Hill Road in Vestal, Riverside Drive in Johnson City & Binghamton, and the Downtown Center in Downtown Binghamton.

LRS

Services Main Street in Johnson City & Binghamton (until Beethoven St), Beethoven Street, Leroy Street & Oak Street in Westside Binghamton, then Court Street & the BC Junction in Downtown Binghamton, and Tompkins Street and Conklin Avenue in Southside Binghamton.

UDC

Services the University Downtown Center in Downtown Binghamton.

CS

Services all campus residential communities as well as the East & West Gyms, the Couper Admin Building, the McGuire Building, Clearview, Academic A, and the Union.

UC

Services UClub in University Plaza, the Meadows & Hayes student communities, as well as the corner of Lehigh Av & Washington Dr at the back entrance of campus.

ITC-UClub

Services the Innovative Technologies Complex, UClub in University Plaza, the Meadows & Hayes student communities, as well as the corner of Lehigh Av & Washington Dr at the back entrance of campus.

RRT

Services Vestal Parkway, the Riviera Ridge & Hollybrook living communities, Target & Parkway Plaza, and Walmart & the Town Square Mall.

OAK

Services the Oakdale Commons and Wegmans.
