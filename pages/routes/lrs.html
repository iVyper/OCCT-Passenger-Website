LRS

Leroy Southside

This route leaves campus and travels through Bunn Hill Road in Vestal, Main Street in Johnson City & Binghamton, Beethoven Street, Leroy Street & Oak Street in Westside Binghamton, then Court Street & the BC Junction in Downtown Binghamton, then Tompkins Street and Conklin Avenue in Southside Binghamton enroute back to the main campus.
